### ⭐ Featured Projects

#### [OmniInv-Syn](https://github.com/RosemyneH/OmniInv-Syn)

A sophisticated inventory ecosystem designed specifically for the Synastria world. It provides a unified, powerful way to manage items across your journey.

#### [AttuneHelper](https://github.com/RosemyneH/AttuneHelper)

Never miss an upgrade. This advanced equipment manager automates your gear swaps to ensure you are always wearing valid, attunable items at the right time.

#### [TheJournal](https://github.com/RosemyneH/TheJournal)

Your personal adventure log. An integrated system for tracking milestones and recording your progress through the unique world of Synastria.

---

### 🛠️ Synastria UI Enhancements

*   **[qtBar](https://github.com/RosemyneH/qtBar)**: Visualizes item attunement as a fun experience bar. Watch your gear level up with you!
*   **[cDF — Synastria](https://github.com/RosemyneH/cDF---Synastria)**: A framework fork supporting **Dual Class** systems via custom class masks.
*   **[Talented Synastria](https://github.com/RosemyneH/talented-synastria)**: Specialized talent management with custom profile imports and exports.
*   **[qtRunner](https://github.com/RosemyneH/qtRunner) & [qtRoll](https://github.com/RosemyneH/qtRoll)**: Lightweight performance modules for execution and loot.
